Movenpick Hotel Karachi

Club Road, Karachi, Pakistan

Movenpick Hotel Karachi offers 5-star accommodation in Karachi. Guests can also make use of complimentary wireless internet. There are a range of facilities on offer to guests of the hotel, such as a coffee bar, an executive floor and valet parking. A fitness centre can be readily accessed by guests wishing to keep up their exercise regimes while travelling. Movenpick Hotel Karachi's 407 luxury rooms provide a mini bar, a private bathroom and bottled water, plus all the essentials for a comfortable stay. Hot drinks can be brewed with complimentary coffee and tea supplies. Those looking for a meal and wanting to stay nearby needn't look any further than Movenpick Hotel Karachi's on-site restaurant, which is ideally available each day for dinner. Jinnah International Airport is a 35-minute drive from the hotel.
